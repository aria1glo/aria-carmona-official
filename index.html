This is Aria Carmona

Aria Carmona is a 21 year old female model with the ambitions of making waves in the industry. The Manhattan-based model is a Barbizon Modeling Development prospect, an engineering student, and licensed NASCAR driver. Modeling is her primary passion, and expanding her portfolio of experience is her objective for the moment. She comes with a multitude of photoshoot experience with brands such as Krissy King, Fifth & Fierce, and Financial Freedom Apparel. She also comes with runway experience, having walked in shows during Miami Swim Week and in New York Swim Week this year. She now looks to enhance her modeling career and take it to the next level.
