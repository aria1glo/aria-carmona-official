Biography

Aria Carmona is a 21 year old female model with the ambitions of making waves in the industry. The Manhattan-based model comes with experience as a Barbizon Modeling Development prospect, since November 2024. She is also a college student studying engineering, and a licensed NASCAR driver. Modeling is her primary passion and expanding her portfolio of experience is her objective for the moment. She comes with a multitude of photoshoot experience with the likes of Alex Dani and Karla Delorve. She also comes with runway experience, having walked in Miami Swim Week 2025 for Frisk Me Good’s Swimwear Line. She was also present in Dallas Fashion Week in March 2025, as she looks to enhance her modeling career and take it to the next level.
