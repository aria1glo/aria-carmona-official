About Aria

Aria Carmona is a 21 year old emerging fashion model making her mark with stunning runway presence, striking features, and unstoppable energy. The Manhattan-based model comes with experience as a Barbizon Modeling Development prospect, since November 2024. Her line of experience includes a multitude of photoshoots, and runway experience in Miami Swim Week 2025. Follow her journey as she graces shows, shoots editorials, and sets trends.
