About Aria

Aria Carmona is a 21 year old female model with the ambitions of making waves in the industry. The Manhattan-based model is a Barbizon Modeling Development prospect, an engineering student, & a licensed NASCAR driver. Modeling is her primary passion & expanding her portfolio of experience is her objective for the moment. She comes with a multitude of photoshoot experience with brands such as Krissy King, Fifth & Fierce, and Financial Freedom Apparel. She also comes with runway experience, having walked in Miami Swim Week 2025 for Frisk Me Good’s Swimwear Line & in New York Swim Week 2025 for MLC Swimwear. She now looks to enhance her modeling career and take it to the next level.
