Biography

Aria Carmona is a 21-year-old female model with ambitions of making waves in the industry. The Manhattan-based model comes with experience as a Barbizon Modeling Development prospect, since November 2024. She is also a college student studying engineering and a licensed NASCAR driver. Modeling is her primary passion, and expanding her portfolio of experience is her objective for the moment. She has a multitude of photoshoot experience with the likes of Alex Dani and Karla Delorve. Aria has also walked in Miami Swim Week 2025 for Frisk Me Good’s Swimwear Line and participated in Dallas Fashion Week 2025, taking her career to the next level.
